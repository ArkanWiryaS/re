# BAB II: ANALISIS PENGGUNA DAN TUGAS (USER & TASK ANALYSIS)

## 2.1 Identifikasi Target Users

### 2.1.1 Overview Ekosistem Pengguna

Platform Verdant dirancang untuk melayani ekosistem **sustainable industrial development** yang kompleks, dimana berbagai stakeholder memiliki peran dan kebutuhan yang saling berkaitan namun berbeda. Berdasarkan analisis mendalam terhadap market landscape dan stakeholder mapping, identifikasi user groups dilakukan melalui pendekatan **multi-tier user classification** yang membedakan tingkat keterlibatan dan pengaruh terhadap platform.

**Framework Klasifikasi Pengguna:**

- **Primary Users:** Stakeholder utama yang secara langsung menggunakan core functionality platform
- **Secondary Users:** Stakeholder pendukung yang mendapat manfaat dari platform ecosystem
- **Tertiary Users:** Stakeholder peripheral yang terpengaruh oleh aktivitas platform

### 2.1.2 Primary Users Analysis

#### **A. Innovators (Innovation Creators & Technology Developers)**

**Definisi Operasional:**
Individu atau organisasi yang mengembangkan solusi teknologi inovatif untuk mengatasi tantangan industrial dengan fokus pada sustainability dan alignment dengan Sustainable Development Goals, khususnya SDG 9 (Industry, Innovation, and Infrastructure).

**Sub-segmentasi Innovators:**

**1. Startup Founders (Tech Entrepreneurs)**

- **Age Range:** 21-35 tahun
- **Background:** Technical education (engineering, computer science, environmental science)
- **Characteristics:** High risk tolerance, technology-savvy, sustainability-minded
- **Primary Motivation:** Scaling sustainable technology solutions, accessing funding ecosystem
- **Pain Points:** Limited access to appropriate investors, difficulty demonstrating technology value

**2. Healthcare Professionals (Medical Researchers & Practitioners)**

- **Age Range:** 25-45 tahun
- **Background:** Medical education dengan interest di digital health dan workplace safety
- **Characteristics:** Evidence-based approach, regulatory compliance awareness, patient safety focus
- **Primary Motivation:** Implementing AI solutions untuk healthcare improvement, workplace safety innovation
- **Pain Points:** Technology adoption barriers, regulatory complexity, funding for research translation

**3. University Students (Graduate Researchers)**

- **Age Range:** 22-28 tahun
- **Background:** Advanced degree programs (Master's/PhD) dalam relevant technical fields
- **Characteristics:** Research-oriented, limited commercial experience, academic approach
- **Primary Motivation:** Translating research into practical applications, gaining industry exposure
- **Pain Points:** Research-to-market gap, limited business development skills, networking limitations

#### **B. Investors (Capital Providers & Financial Partners)**

**Definisi Operasional:**
Individu atau institusi yang menyediakan financial resources untuk sustainable technology development dengan ekspektasi return on investment yang mencakup financial returns dan measurable social/environmental impact.

**Sub-segmentasi Investors:**

**1. Impact Investors (Professional Impact Investment Firms)**

- **Age Range:** 30-50 tahun
- **Background:** Finance, investment management, sustainable development
- **Characteristics:** ESG-focused, long-term perspective, impact measurement emphasis
- **Primary Motivation:** Achieving measurable social/environmental impact alongside financial returns
- **Pain Points:** Quality deal flow scarcity, impact measurement complexity, due diligence challenges

**2. VC Partners (Technology-Focused Venture Capitalists)**

- **Age Range:** 35-55 tahun
- **Background:** Technology, finance, business development
- **Characteristics:** Scalability focus, risk assessment expertise, portfolio management approach
- **Primary Motivation:** Identifying high-growth sustainable technology opportunities
- **Pain Points:** Technical feasibility assessment, market timing evaluation, competitive landscape navigation

**3. Angel Investors (High-Net-Worth Individuals)**

- **Age Range:** 40-65 tahun
- **Background:** Successful entrepreneurs, industry experts, technology leaders
- **Characteristics:** Hands-on involvement, mentorship orientation, sector expertise
- **Primary Motivation:** Supporting next-generation entrepreneurs, leveraging expertise for impact
- **Pain Points:** Time constraints, deal flow quality, post-investment engagement management

## 2.3 Task Analysis

### 2.3.1 Task Analysis Methodology

**Hierarchical Task Analysis (HTA)** digunakan untuk memecah complex user goals menjadi manageable subtasks dan identifying potential usability issues. Metodologi ini dipilih karena kemampuannya untuk:

- **Mengidentifikasi task sequences** yang optimal untuk different user types
- **Menganalisis cognitive load** untuk each task step
- **Identifying error recovery opportunities** dalam user workflows
- **Optimizing task efficiency** melalui interface design

**Task Analysis Framework:**

1. **Goal Identification:** High-level objectives untuk each user type
2. **Task Decomposition:** Breaking goals into specific, measurable tasks
3. **Sequence Analysis:** Optimal task ordering dan dependencies
4. **Error Analysis:** Potential failure points dan recovery mechanisms
5. **Efficiency Assessment:** Time dan effort requirements untuk task completion

### 2.4.3 Edge Case Scenarios

#### **Edge Case 1: Platform Technical Failure During Critical Submission**

**Scenario:** Arief submitting time-sensitive investment application when platform experiences technical difficulties.

**Context:** Application deadline approaching, complete documentation prepared, network connectivity issues encountered.

**Error Recovery Process:**

1. **Automatic Save Functionality:** Platform preserves completed form data
2. **Alternative Submission Methods:** Email backup submission option provided
3. **Deadline Extension:** Automatic extension granted untuk technical difficulties
4. **Support Escalation:** Real-time support chat available untuk urgent issues
5. **Multi-device Continuity:** Form accessible dari different devices dengan same account

#### **Edge Case 2: Investor Due Diligence Information Gap**

**Scenario:** Michael requires additional technical documentation yang not available through standard platform submission.

**Context:** Complex technical evaluation requires specialized documentation, direct technical team access needed.
